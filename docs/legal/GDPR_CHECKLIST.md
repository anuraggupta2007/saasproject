# GDPR Compliance Checklist

**Effective Date:** July 8, 2026
**Last Updated:** July 8, 2026
**Review Frequency:** Quarterly

## Overview

This checklist ensures Email Converter Ltd. ("we," "our," or "us") maintains compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR") and applicable data protection laws. Each item includes the responsible party, implementation status, and evidence of compliance.
